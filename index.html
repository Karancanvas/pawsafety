About Paw Safety

Founded with love and compassion, we're dedicated to creating a world where every stray animal has access to love, care, and a safe home.

Our Mission

At Paw Safety, we believe that every life matters. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome stray animals while promoting responsible pet ownership and reducing overpopulation through education and community outreach.

We provide comprehensive care including emergency medical treatment, nutritious meals, safe shelter, and lots of love to help these beautiful souls heal and find their forever homes.
